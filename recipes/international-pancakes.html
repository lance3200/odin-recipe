International Pancakes

Description

An easy recipe for creating the most loved breakfast meal! Pancakes! Loved for it's lovely gritty taste this recipe will guide you through ingredients and such to help you make the most beautiful pancakes

If you enjoyed this recipe, please use our other recipes to help you cook amazing dishes!

Ingredients!

  • 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 egg
  • 1 1/4 cups buttermilk
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium high heat.
  2. In a large bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking powder and salt. Stir in egg, buttermilk and oil until mixture is smooth.
  3. Pour the batter by spoonfuls into the hot pan, forming 5 inch circles. When the edges appear to harden, flip the pancakes. Cook on both sides until golden brown. Serve hot.
